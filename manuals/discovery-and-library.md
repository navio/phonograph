# Discovery & Library

## Discover New Podcasts

Use the Discover experience to:

- Search by show title or topic.
- Browse curated sections and recommendations.
- Open individual shows to inspect episode lists.

## Save and Organize

When you find a show you like:

1. Open the podcast page.
2. Save or subscribe from the show controls.
3. Confirm it appears in **Library**.

## Episode Browsing

Inside a podcast page, you can:

- Sort through recent episodes.
- Open episode details.
- Add an episode to your playback queue.

## Library Workflow

Your Library is optimized for repeat listening:

- Reopen followed shows quickly.
- Continue episodes where you left off.
- Remove items you no longer want to track.

## Best Practices

- Keep your library focused on active shows.
- Use playlist actions when planning a listening session.
- Revisit Discover periodically for new content.
