# Getting Started

## Accessing Phonograph

- Open the app at `https://phonograph.app`.
- Open the manual directly at `https://phonograph.app/docs/`.

## Core Navigation

Phonograph includes five primary areas:

- **Discover**: browse featured content and recommendations.
- **Library**: view your subscribed and saved podcasts.
- **Podcast**: open a show page and browse episodes.
- **Playlist**: manage your current playback queue.
- **Settings**: configure playback and import/export options.

## Install as an App (PWA)

On supported browsers/devices:

1. Open browser menu.
2. Choose **Install app** or **Add to Home Screen**.
3. Launch Phonograph from your home screen or app launcher.

## First Session Checklist

1. Search for a podcast in Discover.
2. Open a show and pick an episode.
3. Start playback and verify controls in the player bar.
4. Save content to your library for quick return.
